Honored to win **Second Runner-Up in the Intelligent Infrastructure Engineering category** at Iowa State University's 13th Annual CCEE Graduate Student Poster Competition!

Our research team applied machine learning and large language models to forecast pavement durability over extended periods, aiming to transform approaches to infrastructure durability and maintenance scheduling.

#### Research Impact

- Advanced forecasting of pavement durability using ML and LLMs
- Innovative approaches to maintenance scheduling
- Contributing to civil engineering innovation and sustainable infrastructure
